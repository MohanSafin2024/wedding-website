Dress

Smart casual
Look nice, but not too nice! Many of our guests will be travelling long distances with limited suitcase space, so let's keep this occasion classy but casual.
As the ceremony will be held on the beach, be sure to bring comfortable, beach-proof shoes. Dinner will be served around sunset time, so bring something warm! (A typical August day in Tahoe is 79/50F, or 26/10C.)

How we met

We met in 2015 at a 2 week neuroscience course in Florence, Italy. We fell in love inside the classroom and in a cute little plaza where the class went almost every night. The course ended, and after a teary-eyed goodbye, we went our seperate ways thinking we'd never see eachother again. We survived life apart for about 6 hours. Then one message, then another, then thousands more... the rest is history!
